Worry Free Materials

Our mats are made with TPE (Thermoplastic Elastomer), a
durable foam that’s safe for kids and safe for the planet.

TPE is hypoallergenic and free from harmful chemicals such as PVC, Phthalates, Latex and BPA. We have our products CPSC lab certified to ensure the utmost standards in quality and safety.

TPE is more sustainable than other leading materials (such as PVC) used to make yoga mats. Not only do we print our designs with eco-friendly inks, our mats are 100% recyclable and biodegradable.

We use closed-cell technology to help the surface resist moisture and bacteria growth. In addition to making them more wear-resistant, the mats are easy to clean. Just hand wash with mild soap, rinse and air dry.
